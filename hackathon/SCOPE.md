# Project Scope

## Product

**Exam-Cram Assistant** is a Chrome study companion that helps students turn their own sources into grounded visual lessons, practice material, and lasting learning progress.

## Audience

The product is for students across all subjects. It is designed for everyday course learning and for exam revision.

Example subjects include science, mathematics, history, law, languages, business, and computing. The product does not depend on a subject-specific content library; it works from the source material the student deliberately chooses.

## Problem

Students commonly study across web pages, PDFs, lecture videos, and personal notes. The information is fragmented, and generic AI assistance can be difficult to verify or turn into an effective revision routine.

## Core promise

In one study session, a student can save a source, understand it through a source-grounded visual lesson, practice retrieval with a quiz, and identify what to study next.

## In-scope experience

1. Create or select a named study chapter.
2. Save a page, document, note, or supported video source to that chapter.
3. Generate a visual tutor note and source-grounded cheat sheet.
4. Explore key concepts and their supporting evidence.
5. Generate and complete an active-recall quiz.
6. Review progress and weak concepts in the Journey.
7. Export selected study material or start a Focus session when useful.

## Demo scenario

A first-time student opens the zero-setup mathematics route, which saves a short linear-equations source to a dedicated stable chapter, shows an evidence-linked visual note, completes one source-checked question, and sees the result in Journey. The curated mathematics example is only the judge path; the core product remains source-led and subject agnostic.

## Out of scope

- Replacing teachers, learning-management systems, or course materials.
- Claiming that generated material is always correct.
- Building classroom administration, social feeds, or real-time collaboration for this submission.
- Supporting every content platform or provider before the core study loop is clear and reliable.

## Success condition

A first-time student can complete the curated demo loop in 60-90 seconds and understands both the generated material and the evidence that supports it. A normal user-owned source follows the same loop without claiming that every generated statement is automatically correct.
